About
NU:ll

NU:ll is a simulated financial awareness experience.

This project is designed to confront the hidden costs of digital spending.

By simulating the data-access patterns of the apps and platforms we use every day, NULL reveals what impulse purchasing actually adds up to, broken down by category and harder to ignore than you'd expect.

NULL exposes the psychology behind "it's just a small purchase" culture and their one tap payments, the algorithm-driven recommendations, the way digital money never quite feels real.

Recognizing the manipulation is the first step to spending on your own terms.

No real data is collected. Everything you see is generated to feel uncomfortably close to real.
